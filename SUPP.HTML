About AeroTech

Pioneering aerospace education since 1985.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional education in aircraft mechanical engineering, combining rigorous academics with practical, hands-on training that prepares students for successful careers in the aerospace industry.

Our History

Founded by a group of aerospace engineers who saw the need for more practical, industry-focused education in aircraft systems. What began as a small technical school has grown into a leading institution for aerospace education.

Our Facilities

Our 50-acre campus includes wind tunnels, aircraft hangars with operational training aircraft, avionics labs, and structural testing facilities that give students real-world experience.
